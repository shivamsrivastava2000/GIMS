Message

As we reflect on our journey since the inception of the Government Institute of Medical Sciences (GIMS) Greater Noida, I am filled with pride in the remarkable strides we have made in advancing healthcare delivery in our region.

Since our establishment on the 15th of February, 2016, GIMS has been steadfast in its commitment to enhancing the health and well-being of both rural and urban populations in western Uttar Pradesh. Our vision, rooted in providing state-of-the-art facilities and ensuring equitable access to quality healthcare, has been the guiding force behind every endeavor we undertake.


Under the able leadership of Shri (Dr) Anup Chandra Pandey, Chief Secretary, Govt. of Uttar Pradesh, and Dr. Rajneesh Dube, Principal Secretary Medical Education, we have forged ahead with determination and purpose. Their unwavering support has been instrumental in shaping GIMS into a center of excellence in the field of health delivery system, medical education, and research.


I am privileged to serve as the Director of this esteemed institution, and I extend my heartfelt gratitude to each member of our faculty and staff. Your dedication, coupled with your expertise from renowned institutions, has been pivotal in our journey towards excellence. Our commitment to staying abreast of the latest advancements in medical education technology and adhering to the highest standards set by the Medical Council of India is unwavering.

Located in Greater Noida, near Kasna in Gautam Buddha Nagar, GIMS serves as a beacon of hope for the surrounding districts, including Gautam Buddha Nagar, Hapur, Bulandshehar, Khurja, Jewar, Mathura, and beyond. Our mission to provide comprehensive medical facilities without discrimination is at the core of everything we do.


I am pleased to report that our infrastructure is state-of-the-art, equipped with high-end technology and laboratories, including a dedicated Skill Lab. The hospital, with its comprehensive care in all specialties, stands as a testament to our commitment to serving our community.

As we witness a steady increase in patient footfall, with approximately 1500-2000 patients visiting our OPD daily and over 200 patients admitted to our hospital, it reaffirms the trust our community places in us.


In conclusion, let us continue our journey with renewed vigor, compassion, and dedication. Together, we will overcome challenges, achieve new milestones, and continue to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.
