BOYS HOSTEL

Welcome to Munshi Premchand Boys Hostel at GIMS, situated in the heart of Gautam Buddha University. Named after the esteemed Hindi and Urdu writer, our hostel offers 245 single rooms across four floors, each fully furnished with essential amenities. With water purifiers and cold water dispensers on every floor, we prioritize your comfort and convenience. Join our vibrant community as we foster an environment for academic excellence and personal growth. Welcome to your home away from home.

GIRL'S HOSTEL

Welcome to Ismat Chughtai Girls Hostel at GIMS, within Gautam Buddha University's campus. Named after the renowned Indian writer, our hostel offers 200 single rooms across four well-ventilated floors. Each floor features a water purifier and cold water dispenser for convenience. Our amenities include a recreation room and a reading room with computer facilities. Join us in a supportive community, where academic excellence and personal growth flourish. Welcome to your home away from home.
