Overview of GIMS Hospital

Where healing, teaching and discovery come together

  • Government Institute of Medical Sciences (GIMS) Greater Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar (UP) is an autonomous medical teaching institute established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh on 15th February 2016. GIMS is registered as an autonomous institution under the society and is affiliated with the prestigious Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University, UP.

  • GIMS is situated in Greater Noida, near Kasna, in Gautam Buddha Nagar in the Delhi NCR region, adjacent to the Yamuna Expressway and Eastern Peripheral Expressway, ensuring excellent accessibility and connectivity. Patients from various districts in western Uttar Pradesh, including Gautam Buddha Nagar, Hapur, Baghpat, Meerut, Bulandshehar, Khurja, Jewar, Mathura, and neighboring areas, rely on this hospital for treatment. With the development of Asia's largest airport in Jewar and Gautam Buddha Nagar's status as the industrial hub of Uttar Pradesh, the absence of a tertiary-level government hospital in the region is notable.

  • GIMS was established with the aim of improving the health status of the rural and urban population of western Uttar Pradesh. This is achieved by providing a state-of-the-art health facility and facilitating equitable access to quality healthcare through a revamped healthcare system, targeted outreach services, and involvement of community and local bodies.

  • The vision of the institute is to establish GIMS as a "Centre of Excellence in Patient Care, Medical Education, and Biomedical Research." Currently, there are over 100 ongoing research projects, including 11 extramural projects sponsored by organizations such as ICMR, CSIR, WHO, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, and Ayush. Additionally, since August 1, 2019, the institute has been conducting an MBBS course for 100 students. Furthermore, it offers postgraduate courses (DNB) in Medicine, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Pediatrics, and Anesthesia, accredited by the National Board of Examinations.

  • The institute boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modular operation theaters, well-equipped high-end equipment, NABL-accredited laboratories, and a highly advanced Skill Lab. With a capacity of 500 beds, the hospital is fully functional, providing comprehensive care across all broad specialties. The patient load in both the outpatient department (OPD) and inpatient department is steadily increasing, with approximately 1500-2000 patients visiting daily and over 350 patients admitted to the hospital. In 2019 alone, approximately 427,712 patients were reported, with 11,825 patients admitted to the hospital.

    The hospital's core mission is to provide comprehensive healthcare for individuals with routine or severe health issues, whether stemming from injury, disease, or social factors. Operating 24/7, year-round, it brings together physicians across specialties, efficient administrators, and advanced equipment to deliver top-quality care to those with acute and chronic conditions. Services include emergency care, surgeries, labor and delivery, diagnostics, lab tests, and patient education. Patients may receive inpatient or outpatient care based on their health needs, ensuring tailored and holistic healthcare solutions for their well-being and recovery.
