Online OPD Booking

Government Institute of Medical Sciences (GIMS), Greater Noida has emerged as a center of excellence in delivering world-class tertiary patient care facilities in Gautam Buddha Nagar at affordable prices. The Institute now offers digital journey towards well-being with the implementation of safe and convenient “online OPD booking” services. This pre-online registration will allay fear of long queues, enable smooth monitoring of patient flow and encourage COVID appropriate behavior.

For Online OPD Booking Follow the following simple steps:

  • Visit the website www.gims.ac.in
  • Click on online registration
  • Verify using Aadhar card
  • Select GIMS hospital
  • Choose the department
  • Date of appointment will be received as message on the registered mobile no.
